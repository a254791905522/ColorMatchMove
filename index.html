Color Match Move

Physics puzzles · Shelves & conveyors · Themed worlds

Free Puzzle iPhone & iPad Ages 4+

Color Match Move is a bright, physics-based puzzle game about clearing shelves the smart way. Tap objects to drop them onto a limited-slot conveyor, line them up with the matching colored bins below, and clear the board before the queue backs up. Every move counts—plan ahead so you never block a match you still need.

Journey through themed levels from deep space to snowy forests. New twists appear as you go: odd shapes, moving belts, and clever obstacles that keep each stage fresh.

📦

Shelf physics

Objects tumble and stack realistically—use gravity and timing to your advantage.

🎯

Color-to-bin matching

Only the right hue in the right place clears the puzzle—watch the conveyor slots.

🌌

Easy to pick up, satisfying to master—perfect for a break or a longer run.
