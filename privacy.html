Built for play, not profiling

Color Match Move is a casual puzzle experience. This page explains what happens with information when you use the game.

Publisher

Who provides this game

Color Match Move is offered by Aaqib Javed. This privacy statement applies to the mobile app distributed on Apple’s App Store and to this promotional site when it describes the same product.

Collection

What we collect from you

We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal data through Color Match Move. That includes:

  • Name, email, phone number, or social accounts
  • Precise or coarse location
  • Advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking signals
  • Diagnostics, crash reports, or analytics tied to your identity (we do not run such collection in-app)
  • Gameplay telemetry uploaded to our servers (we do not operate gameplay data backends for this title as described here)
If Apple’s systems process limited technical data when you download or update the app (for example billing or fraud prevention), that processing is governed by Apple’s policies, not by us as app data collection.

Local only

What stays on your device

Like many puzzle games, Color Match Move may store progress, preferences, or similar settings locally on your phone or tablet. That information remains on the device unless the operating system provides its own sync or backup features outside our control.

Removing the app typically deletes app-specific local data. Restoring from a device backup may bring saved progress back—again, that is handled by your device and Apple’s ecosystem, not by us collecting it remotely.

Ecosystem

App Store & external pages

Purchases, updates, and reviews go through Apple. Their privacy rules apply to those interactions. If you open links (for example to the App Store listing or a hosted privacy document), the destination site’s policy governs that visit.
